Why HIIT Workouts?

High intensity interval training (HIIT) is a great way to get a good workout in a short amount of time. These workouts are comprised of interval training, which means exercises are completed in quick, intense bursts, followed by short, often active, recovery periods. HIIT workouts keep your heart rate up, making it a time-efficient way to burn some calories.

Another great aspect of HIIT workouts is that they can be done anywhere! Whether you are at home or at the gym, you will always be able to complete HIIT exercises. Additionally, these workouts generally do not require equipment, making it even easier to do one your own.
